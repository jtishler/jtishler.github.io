Sketchy
Oil paint, p5.js program
3' x 4.5'

Directions:

  1. Draw shapes and lines to contribute to this piece.
  2. Press enter to save your canvas to your computer.
  3. I'd love to see your creation! You can send it to jenna@tishler.net.


Controls:

  • Press 'r' for rectangle creation.
  • Press 'e' for ellipse creation.
  • Press 'l' for line drawing.
  • Press 's' for shape selection.
  • Press 'c' for to change the color of the selected shapes to the current color picker color.
  • Hold shift and drag to rotate selected shape.
  • Hold control and drag to rotate selected shape.
  • Press delete to remove selected shape.
  • Press escape to clear canvas.

View this website on a non-mobile device at full width to see Sketchy piece.
